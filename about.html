About AndroUnity

Introduction

In 2017, a group of android fan-a-techs came together to form a community now known as "AndroUnity". Based on the common agenda of discussing Android Device along with news, updates and feeds from various other technologies dependent or independent of the android market.

What we do

The community being a mix of people from different background, age and experiences come up with innovative ideas to solve problems, design apps for all type of needs, icon packs and the overall satisfaction of the android community. Besides these as an appreciation of the love we receive we do giveaways of various products or icon packs in collaboration with the makers, make icon packs, subtratum themes and widgets for the fanbase. You can find them here.
